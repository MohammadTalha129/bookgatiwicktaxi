Book a Gatwick to London City Airport Taxi

Book Gatwick Taxi offers London City Airport transfers 24/7, which guarantees comfortable and fixed-price inter-airport transfers, connecting Gatwick Airport (LGW) with London City Airport (LCY).

This is an important route, especially for people who need to get to London's business and financial districts quickly. We take away the stress of having to change trains and make connections on the DLR by offering a professional, fixed-price transfer from LGW to LCY and a taxi from London City to Gatwick that is available 24/7. Our service is the fastest way to get from Gatwick to the center of the Docklands.

Why Choose Our LGW to LCY Fixed Price Transfer?

Because of the M25 segment and the heavy traffic in East London, getting to London City Airport (LCY) requires special knowledge.

  • Guaranteed Fixed Fares: The price you see when you book is the price you pay. This fixed-price commitment covers all road charges, tolls (e.g., Dartford Tunnel/Silvertown Tunnel route options), taxes, and standard waiting time. Traffic jams around the Blackwall Tunnel or Docklands will not cause the price to go up.
  • We monitor your flight in real time as it lands at both LGW and LCY. Your driver's dispatch time is automatically changed for delays or early arrivals at no extra cost. This makes sure they are ready when you arrive at the Arrivals Hall.
  • Your driver will meet you in the Arrivals Hall of the specified terminal (North/South at LGW; the single terminal at LCY) with a sign with your name on it. They help you with your bags and show you to your executive vehicle.
  • Available 24/7: Our large fleet and experienced drivers are ready to take you safely and quickly from Gatwick to London City, day or night.
  • Direct to the Financial District: We offer direct door-to-door service to Canary Wharf and the City of London, which are the main areas LCY serves. This means you don't have to take the DLR or the Tube.

How long is a taxi from Gatwick to London City Airport?

London City Airport (LCY) and Gatwick Airport (LGW) are about 47–50 miles (76–80 kilometers) apart by road. The route goes through big highways (M23/M25) and city streets (A2/A13/A102).

Important Buffer: If you have a short connecting flight at LCY, we recommend allowing at least 3 to 3.5 hours for the whole process, which includes picking you up, traveling, and checking in.

London City Airport Facts and Pickup Guide

LCY (IATA: LCY) is a unique and compact airport in the Royal Docks. It is the nearest airport to the City of London's financial district and is renowned for its quick check-in times and short walking distances.

London City Airport (LCY) Pick-Up Protocol

  • LCY Single Terminal: Your driver will meet you inside the main Arrivals Hall of this small terminal, usually near the information desk or the main exit, with a sign with your name on it. The speed of LCY helps with quick collection.

Gatwick (LGW) Pick-Up Protocol

  • North Terminal and South Terminal are both served by us. Your driver will meet you in the terminal's Arrivals Hall and take you to the M23.
