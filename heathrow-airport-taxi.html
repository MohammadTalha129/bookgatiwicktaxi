How long is a taxi from Heathrow to Gatwick?

The M25 taxi motorway is mostly used for the roughly 45-mile (72-kilometer) trip. The traffic flow has a significant impact on the transfer time for your private taxi from Heathrow to Gatwick.

All passengers on a connecting flight are advised to allow a minimum of three hours for the entire process, which includes pickup, travel time, and Gatwick (LGW) check-in.

Journey Time Estimates: Heathrow (LHR) to Gatwick (LGW) Taxi

Please note that the estimated transfer time is for the journey itself, while the recommended allocation includes buffer time for collection and potential minor delays.

Off-Peak (midday, midmorning):

  • 45 to 60 minutes is the estimated transfer time.
  • Suggested Allocation: Set aside one hour and fifteen minutes.

Peak Hours: 7-9 am, 4-7 pm

  • 75 to 90 minutes is the estimated transfer time.
  • Suggested Allocation: Set aside one hour and forty-five minutes.

Late at night (11 p.m. to 5 a.m.):

  • 40 to 55 minutes is the estimated transfer time.
  • The suggested allocation is one hour.
