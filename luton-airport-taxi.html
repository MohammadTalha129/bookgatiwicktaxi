How long is a taxi from Gatwick to Luton? (LGW to LTN Journey Details)

Gatwick Airport (LGW) and Luton Airport (LTN) are roughly 70–75 miles (113–120 km) apart. Traffic flow has a significant impact on travel time, particularly on orbital motorways.

We strongly recommend that travelers on a connecting flight allow at least 3.5 to 4 hours for the entire process, which includes check-in at the destination terminal, the actual journey, and collection.
